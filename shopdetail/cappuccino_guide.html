Cappuccino
About this coffee

The perfect balance of bold espresso and warm milk

Temperature
93°C
Milk level text
Full
Time
3:00
Grind
Medium
Instructions
1-Grind 18g of coffee (fine setting).
2-Extract 36g of espresso into a cup.
3-Steam 150ml milk to 65°C (create microfoam).
4-Swirl pitcher gently to smooth out bubbles.
5-Pour milk steadily over espresso to fill.
Serve and enjoy.

Slowing down the world, one cup at a time. Experience clarity and let the minute stay.
